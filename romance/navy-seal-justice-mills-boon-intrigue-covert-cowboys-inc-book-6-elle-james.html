FBI agent Melissa Bradley is used to cryptic assignments like this: retrieve package from P.O. Box and deliver to old friend. But package in hand, she’s attacked by thugs and not about to trust the tall, dark and dangerous rescuer who claims he was sent to protect her. A former Navy SEAL turned cowboy suddenly reinstated as a SEAL, there’s clearly more to James Monahan’s covert op than he’ll admit. When their mutual friend goes missing, they join forces deep in the swamplands of southern Mississippi to find him. But as a dangerous band of traitors, terrorists and killers close in, survival means trusting each other—their toughest mission yet.
