If I asked you to name all of the things you love, how long would it take you to name yourself?

If you’re anything like the vast majority of humanity, you most likely wouldn’t make it onto your own list. And, there's certainly no lack of reasons as to why you wouldn't -  your father walked on you, your mother gave you up for adoption, your spouse cheated on you, your congregation “voted” you out, your friends betrayed you, your mentor told you it’s wrong, your religion taught you that in order to truly love God then you have to hate or despise yourself. All somewhat convincing explanations. However, none were convincing enough for God to remove us from His list.

Have you ever considered that maybe it's time to re-think your list?  Maybe even re-write it to look more like His?  

With a body of life experiences marked and marred by abandonment and rejection, author, Chevis Brooks, knows all-too-well the harsh reality of feeling unlovable.  The impact often leaves us with three unanswered questions:

  • Who Am I?
  • What Am I Worth?
  • Why Am I Here?

Through a powerful process, In God's Eyes, will show you how to:

  • Recover Your Heavenly Identity
  • Receive Your Heavenly Inheritance
  • Release Your Heavenly Influence
