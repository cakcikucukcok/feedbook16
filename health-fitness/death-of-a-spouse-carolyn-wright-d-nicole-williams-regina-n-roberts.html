Death of a Spouse

Carolyn Wright was born in the beautiful state of Georgia. She is a native of Valdosta. Her family relocated to the city of Jacksonville, Florida when she was 13 years old. She met her husband, Otis Wright, at New Stanton Senior High School in the year of 1958. They were married three years later in 1961. Together, they had three children who then gave birth to seven grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. In marriage, the couple lived through a variety of intricate experiences, some of which are touched upon in this book. Take the time to read through the details of Lady Carolyn Wright’s home life. You may find that this story helps you come to a fuller understanding of your own.

About The Author: Carolyn Wright

This is my story... These pages describe the last few months of time when I lived by my husband’s side during his very short battle with lung cancer. Read the intimate pages of my personal journal, and walk with me as I tell the story of my desire for my husband to find salvation through our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.
