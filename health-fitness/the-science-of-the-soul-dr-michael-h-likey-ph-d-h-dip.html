The Science of the Soul presents Dr. Michael Likeys complete guide to pursuing and excelling at whatever task or goal you may chose. The wisdom offered in The Science of the Soul is designed not only for studying and for practicing, but also for integrating into ones everyday living. The ideas that Dr. Likey discusses have been tested over the years by many of the worlds greatest seers, teachers, authors, and coaches. Spiritual laws are merely metaphors for the natural laws; though unseen, that are always at work in the world around us. The Science of the Soul offers a path for a deeper connection and the transformation of the soul. Using the methods presented here, you can gain greater quality of life, deeper love, and inner peace, focused on your true life purposes.
