In the past some have tended to emphasize creativity at the expense of wisdom or vice versa. In Creative Wisdom you will discover that there is a profound connection between creativity and wisdom. This book is also a valuable tool to help you uncover, discover, stimulate and grow creativity insights through the instrumentality of wisdom.
