You Guard My Heart

Lord, God, Creator and giver of life,

you guard my heart!

In countless ways, through all my days,

you, O breath of life,

you guard my heart!

Trouble comes,

you guard my heart!

People hurt,

you guard my heart!

Worries crawl,

you guard my heart!

O Lord, the living, breathing word,

awesome, unfathomable one,

it is you, only you who can fully guard my heart!

Thank you.

Amen.
