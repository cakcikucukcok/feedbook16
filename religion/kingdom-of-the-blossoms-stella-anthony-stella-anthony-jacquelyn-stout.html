KINGDOM OF THE BLOSSOMS is an eternally triumphant saga about human striving for happiness and truth. The book is a living conglomerate of prose, poetry and visualization, waiting to be discovered by the reader of any age.

GLORY! PEACE! LOVE! UNBOUND RESOURCES!

These are in constant, feverish demand--the quest and search by every human soul that has lived since humanity first existed. Their domain is called with different names by different people: a parallel reality, a field of Intention, The Promised Land. The Holy Bible--the book of my life, calls it God's Kingdom; I know its presence from experience.

"Kingdom of the Blossoms" emanates from the story of God's Kingdom--its reality and energy. It doesn't matter how often one reads the book--the Kingdom's magnetism is always there!

Princess Little Star had the Kingdom and lost it. This is how the great journey began. Did she finally discover the Kingdom again?
