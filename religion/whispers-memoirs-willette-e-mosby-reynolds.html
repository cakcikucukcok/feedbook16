Sometimes you may feel a gentle nudge or hear a soft voice directing the way. Whispers, a seven-day devotional, encourages you to draw closer to our Lord and Savior, assures you that youre not on this journey alone, and inspires you to keep on in spite of everything. In Memoirs, author Willette Mosby-Reynolds shares specific true-life experiences to create an awareness of the fact that God is real, he cares, and he provides. She gives heartfelt accounts of a mothers love for her children, the ups and downs experienced throughout their lives, dreams deferred, hopes dashed, and faith testedall part of the process to improve, recover, and eventually, celebrate the better opportunity. Both will encourage and inspire you or someone you know. Find points of similarity to your own life experiences or become awakened by the diversity of others; seek to identify your personal inner strength, and help others in their quest for peace, comfort, and strength on this journey of life.
