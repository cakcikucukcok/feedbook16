Christians believe that prayer is the breath of the soul -- but the reality is that we are all beginners when it comes to the practice of prayer. We need to learn from the master of prayer, Jesus Christ. By studying his prayers as recorded in the Gospels, the existential significance of prayer and its essentials can be freshly caught. Laszlo Gallusz investigates exegetically Jesus' seven* prayers in their literary and socio-historical setting, and points to their theological meaning and relevance for today.

(* the prayers from the cross are treated as one, as they are in the same context)
