Burnout or tired marriage is real. Some married couples are tired of the same routine they find themselves at home. Some spouses complain of being stressed in their marriage and complain continuously without much attention from their partners. But they are not alone. Several marriages are stressed.

Some couples also feel bored with each other. But they keep on going in the marriage pretending that everything is perfect. Marital tiredness has now gained so much currency. The issue is that, there is not so much information about marital burnout which is a real threat to many marriages.

Some couples forget to go out and enjoy life because of the daily boring routine sets in and breaks their relationship up. Some find themselves in a hopeless situation because they genuinely do not know what to do to rekindle their marriage.

This book, ‘Burn-out: A Major Threat To Marriages,’ is written to inform and educate Christian couples about the reality of marital burnout. It provides the necessary information to help people who are tired in the marriage. The book stresses the importance of communication with emphasis on listening. It also explains the issue of tiredness and how it affects sex and intimacy in the marriage and how they interrelate and affects the marriage and what can be done.

This book also equips you with the necessary information so that with some real effort, the spark in the marriage can be rekindled, as well as     how to bring a sense of adventure and gestures of courtship back into the relationship.
