This book is a thought-provoking piece that has the power to make a very difficult subject simple and easy to understand. The will of man is no less and no more than mans God-given right to choose between right and wrong, good or evil. What this book provides is the truth about the process and a revealing look at the exact image of that will. This book is a direct path to the beginning of the creation of man and God's original image of the inner man and the image of his perfectly designed man. The highlights of the book are as revealing as the details of the arguments put forth by the author. This is a captivating, mind challenging, soul stirring and complete conversation for everyone to enjoy for years to come. The book is full of surprises; it is guaranteed to hold the reader's attention and will be a reference for future reading. The will of man must be accepted in order for one to understand what it is and the role it plays in ones life.
