No matter what we think we know about Jesus Christ, there is always something more to learn about our Savior.

Little Hinges is a heartwarming, eye-opening story of one persons epic attempt to explain the love of God and to provide a way for everyone to live with Him forever in heaven. Combining the four Gospels, author Lee Merrick shares the chronological story of Jesus Christ. Rich in the customs and expectations of the first-century Middle East, the book carefully explains:

who Jesus was and why He came to earth the first time;

why He opposed the Jewish religious leaders;

why He didnt reveal His full identity to His disciples in the very beginning of His ministry;

what caused His own people to turn against Him;

why He was crucified and why He is coming back;

what we should do now to avoid being left behind when He returns for His followers.

Youll also learn why its unnecessary to worry about todays chaotic world.

Little Hinges is for Christians, non-Christians, saints, and sinners. Its historic, prophetic, and uplifting. Everyone can benefit from Little Hinges.
