Do you struggle with having a consistent daily time with God, reading the Bible, and in prayer? This thirty-day devotional book will help you get established in having a daily quiet time with God. Each day consists of a nugget of truth for practical Christian living, a Bible reading passage, and suggestions for a time of prayer.

In addition to helping you develop the habit of reading Gods Word and praying every day, the devotional nuggets will provide you with direction for growing in your Christian walk. These nuggets of truth include the following topics:

Having a daily time with God

Dealing with anxiety

Handling conflict

Overcoming tormenting thoughts

Forgiving

Being content

Memorizing Scriptures

This book is great for new believers or any Christian who wants to establish a daily time of Bible reading and prayer. The result? Consistent daily growth in your relationship with the Lord!
