This story is filled with fun and excitement and a big surprise. Six year old Zoey is celebrating her birthday. Zoey and her friends love animals. She wanted to surprise them with a pet animal to join in the birthday fun. There is no space at her parents home for such a party. Her grandparents are delighted to have her over and celebrate her birthday. She has nothing in mind for a pet animal. Her Grandpa is willing to take her to the petting zoo so she can choose a pet for the day. Zoey is excited to go to the petting zoo to look at all the animals. At the zoo, she made her choice for a pet animal and was anxious to get back home and get dressed for her party. See how she turned the party into a wild zoo and everyone was surprised.
