Amy is a young girl that lives with her parents in Ireland and she has heard stories about fairies all of her life. Amy has always wished to talk to them because she had so many questions to ask and no one could tell her the answers. Her Uncle lives in the country and has beautiful flower gardens and has told her stories of fairies being seen there. This year she was old enough to make the trip to her Uncles alone and her mom and dad said ok for her to visit during a school vacation.

During her visit you have the chance to meet a few fairies that were able to take her on an unforgettable adventure. She gets the answer to over sixty questions while visiting their realm and becomes what they call a near fairy. While in their realm she meets and talks to the fairy council then discovers another unknown being.

My Interview With a Fairy is an easy read, entertaining adventures for all ages, informative and colorful. The questions answered in this book were collected for over eighteen months from all walks of life.
