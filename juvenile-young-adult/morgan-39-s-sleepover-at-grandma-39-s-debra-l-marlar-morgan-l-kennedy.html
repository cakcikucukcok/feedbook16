I'm so excited to let everyone know that "Morgans's Sleepover at Grandma's" is now available as an iPad App. You can purchase the App at the iTunes Store for just $1.99. Many of the pages have an optional Paint Me feature for children to finger paint the pages and create their own custom version of the book. Additional features: Professional voice over, table of contents control to navigate easily through the book and an audio on/off control. I hope this new way of sharing a children's book will add to your children's learning experience. For an autographed copy of Morgan's Sleepover at Grandma's, go to my website at www.debbiedooscreations.com

Having a Grandchild spend the night is a great way to create a lifelong bond. Knowing what to do while spending that time with Grandchildren is another story. But Grandma Marlar had it all figured out ahead of time. Read how Morgan and this Grandma spent the evening together. To make this book more special, Morgan has illustrated the cover, and two other illustrations for Morgan's Sleepover at Grandma's.
