Hannah loves to play with her friends but when winter comes Hannah and her friends run into a problem.  The animals personalities shine and come to life as they add their talents to solve the problem.  The story line is very uplifting and sparks the imagination of children as they try to help solve the problem in the barnyard.  Slippers for Hannah is a story of love and friendship as Harold, Ernie, Reno,  and the rest of the animals in the barnyard work together.  They know that if they work together they can come up with a solution to their problem. The fun part is figuring out which one of these animals is your favorite.  It is hard to settle on just one.  Come along and join the barnyard adventure.  A story that can be enjoyed over and over.  The ending will surprise you.
