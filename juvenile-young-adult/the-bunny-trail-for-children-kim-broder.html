The adventures of Giselle and her family in this Bunny Trail 

book will inspire and enrich the lives of your children. 

 

“Can we play the Gratitude Game?” says Giselle.

“Yes, let’s play,” says Aunt Kim.

 

This simple game encourages children to be grateful for all the wonderful people and 

events in their lives.The stories in this book can also help your children deal with the 

sometimes difficult and confusing situations in their lives by inspiring them to make positive 

choices and feel confident in their abilities. In “The Family Camping Trip,” we meet Ayla, 

who knows she can do anything if she first decides it in her mind:

 

“I am going to set up this tent by myself,” Ayla tells her brother Benjamin. 

Then she sets it up! By following the simple instructions, children will learn to play 

the empowering and creative “I Am” game to help make anything they want come true! 

Filled with fun illustrations, The Bunny Trail for Children can be read for enjoyment,

 and it can also be used as a valuable teaching tool.
