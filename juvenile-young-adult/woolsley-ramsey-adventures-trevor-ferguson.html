One day Woolsey and Ramsey were lying in the field under a tree. Out of the sun it felt cool in the shade. They looked around at the rest of the sheep and lambs jumping about and eating the grass when Woolsey says look at all them idiots running around, way to warm for all that motion man!. Yeah dude says Ramsey not good, lets just rest a while. Hey man lets go down to the big meadow with all the long juicy grass beside the river we could get a cool drink when were there Woolsey suggests. Yeah dude, excellent idea, lets go says Ramsey. Not just yet, says Woolsey, we dont want the rest to follow us - so not cool! So what should we do then, dude? Ramsey replies. I know! says Woolsey if we just slip away through those trees well be gone Before they notice! Cool, great idea dude lets get outta here quick smart! Replies Ramsey.
