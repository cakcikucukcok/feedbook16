When a scan at fourteen weeks clearly reveals a set of natural triplets Eddie and Gill's lives were about to be changed forever by their early arrival. The story follows the humorous and sometimes chaotic happenings when the triplets, with their powers of 'triplepathy' quickly learn how three heads put together can achieve far more mischief than one. It's all a matter of being one step ahead of them, but this is harder than ever imaginable, especially when they are joined by a little brother. Gill soon realises that her poor academic record could be the down fall in this most complex part of her education, as she struggles to study for 'A Masters in Motherhood,' awarded from the 'University of Life.' With Gill and Eddie's involvement in the equine world and love of the countryside, the triplets have a free, fun and varied early few years, which the story follows.
