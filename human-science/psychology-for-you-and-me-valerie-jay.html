In more detail, Part 1 Thinking. Positive Psychology, explains what worry is, why we need it and how, with controlled thinking, we can develop a calm resilience within a constructive lifestyle.

Part 2 Strategies. Therapeutic Psychology, illustrates varied human activities where specific therapies based in biology are presented in an everyday framework that can be adapted individually.

Part 3 Insight. Evolutionary-Biological Psychology, ventures further into neuroscience to examine how we evolved to become the 21st century Homo sapiens of brilliant intelligence. The in-depth searching of Part 3 probes a range of disciplines from Cosmology to Epigenetics, supported with illustrations for such diverse chapters as The immensity of life and us and How to handle our biology.
