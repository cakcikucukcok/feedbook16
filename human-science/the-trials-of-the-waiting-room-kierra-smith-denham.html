What happens in the Waiting Room?

You’re in the Waiting Room because you have an appointment! You’ve taken

the necessary steps to see the physician to address issues that concern you!

The longer you’re required to wait your turn, you grow agitated and

irritated. You’ve flipped through the channels on the TV, read the magazines,

and wasted money in the vending machines. Now, people who came in after

you have already been called to the back.

It seems like everyone’s needs are being met, except yours. Considering

the Master Physician is in appointments with other patients, you can’t see

Him, and you can’t demand His attention. You can either leave a message,

reschedule, or wait!

The Waiting Room represents how close you are to resolution, but not close

enough for a diagnosis or a cure!

Join Kierra and DeLoni Denham as God made them wait, before He

addressed their petition to conceive a child!
