"I felt like I was in an episode of Pretty Little Liars. I had never liked that show much."

Fifteen year old Stella Johnson thinks she has the perfect life:Good friends, loving parents, great grades, and a boyfriend who adores her. But Stella has a few secrets... stuff that she didn't even know existed.

Seven years ago she witnessed a horrible tragedy that made her bury her past and never look back. But when one of her best friends is murdered, the memories start coming back to haunt her.

Stella is overwhelmed by the death of her friend, but still determined to get justice, so when she discovers that her past might be connected to the present, she decides to look back one last time.

But will she discover the truth? Or will she find herself falling into darkness?
