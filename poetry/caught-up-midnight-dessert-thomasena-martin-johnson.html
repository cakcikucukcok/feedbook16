Caught Up Midnight Dessert is a collection of poems that speak to the lover in you. They may say things you dont have the courage to say. They let you live a risqu existence without you really getting out there. It is the magic that comes to life at midnight; it is the song your heart sings to mine that only you and I can hear. It Is passion that sizzles, it is touchable molten lava, it is raw emotions that we hide everyday. It is what Etta James sings about; it is what Barry White is talking about; it is what James Brown is begging for; it is what Betty Wright is crying about; it is what Jimmy Reed and Al Green are asking about,O those Flamingos croone when they say I Only Have Eyes For You. Follow your heart; it has all the answers. If you can identify with what I am saying, then you have been in love or CAUGHT UP IN MIDNIGHT DESSERT.
