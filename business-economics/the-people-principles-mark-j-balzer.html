The book is a leadership fable that follows a recent college hire's trials and tribulation as he enters the work force as a supervisor at a third party distribution company. The author effectively iilustrates the difference of being a manager and a leader and descibes the process of the transformation. Chapters on M&M's, green beans, fishing lures, Rosetta Stone, Milk Bones, Twenty Dollar Bills, S+A=B+O, and Paying it forward are used to make key learning points that allows reader to understand these key concepts and how to apply them to real life applications.
