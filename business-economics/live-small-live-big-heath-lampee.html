Many success stories start with an outlier status. Outliers have harder paths. They have minimal desire to be normal. They dream big. They fail big. They succeed bigger. They believe in their work.

As an inexperienced, debt-laden dentist at twenty-five, Dr. Heath Lampee set off to do the impossible. He wanted to work because it was his choice, not his obligation. He wanted to change people’s lives through dentistry. But how do you do that when you are over a million dollars in debt, with almost no clients, and, like most medical professionals, a slave to insurance companies? Learn the Live Small, Live Big philosophy and see how to do the same and better in any business. By reaching for the impossible, you may just get there.
